Who am I

I am the Head of External Affairs at IBS Software, with over 13 years of experience in industry leadership, government engagement, and ecosystem development. I have worked closely with the Government of Kerala’s High Power IT Advisory Committee, leading efforts in investment promotion, policy coordination, and international outreach.

Earlier, as CEO of GTECH and during my time at CII, I collaborated with industry leaders, academia, media, and government bodies, representing Kerala’s technology ecosystem at national and global platforms.

In my current role, I focus on strengthening IBS Software’s brand presence and building strategic relationships across key stakeholders, enhancing its visibility and credibility in India and globally.

Outside of work, I enjoy travelling, blogging/vlogging, and exploring conversations around innovation and digital transformation.
